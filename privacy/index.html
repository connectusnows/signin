If you disagree with this Privacy Policy's terms, please refrain from using our Services.

This may include but is not limited to your name, email address, postal address, phone number, date of birth, payment information, and any other details you share with us.

3.1 Providing and Improving Services: We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve our Services. 3.4 Legal Compliance and Protection: We may use and disclose your personal information as applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes require.
