Cookie Policy

At Kelltan Ads LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), we value your privacy. We are committed to providing transparency regarding the use of cookies and comparable tracking technologies when you visit or enter our website or utilize our services. This Cookie Policy explains how we employ these technologies to enhance your experience. By accessing our website or using our services, you explicitly consent to the utilization of cookies as outlined in this policy.

1. Understanding Cookies

Cookies are minute text files that are stored on your end in your computer or device whenever you visit a website. These files serve various purposes, including improving your browsing experience, analyzing website usage patterns, and delivering personalized content and advertisements.

2. Our Cookie Categorie

2.1 Essential Cookies: These cookies are vital for the proper functioning of our website and enable you to navigate and utilize its features seamlessly. Typically, they are set in response to your specific actions, such as logging in or completing forms. Without these cookies, certain parts of the website may not operate correctly.

2.2 Performance Cookies: We employ these cookies to gather information about how visitors engage or interact with our website, such as the pages most frequently visited and any encountered error messages. This knowledge aids us in enhancing the performance and usability of our website.

2.3 Customization Cookies: Our website employs these cookies to remember your preferences, allowing us to provide enhanced features and personalized experiences. For instance, we may use these cookies to remember your preferred language or region.

2.4 Advertising Cookies: Collaborating with trusted third-party advertising companies, we utilize these cookies to display relevant advertisements on our website and other platforms you visit. These cookies facilitate the delivery of ads tailored to your interests and browsing history while also assisting in measuring the efficacy of our advertising campaigns.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most web browsers are initially set to accept cookies automatically. However, you retain the ability to adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or receive notifications when cookies are sent. It is important to note that disabling or rejecting cookies may impact the functionality of certain parts of our website. For comprehensive guidance on managing cookies, please refer to your browser's help or settings page. Additionally, you can find useful information on controlling cookies on third-party websites.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may incorporate cookies from third-party service providers, such as analytics or advertising companies. These third parties may gather information about your online activities across different websites over time. Please be aware that we have no control over these third-party cookies, and their respective privacy policies govern their usage.

5. Modifications to Our Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify our Cookie Usage Policy at any time. Any amendments made will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy on our website. By continuing to access our website or utilize our services after such modifications, you acknowledge and accept the updated Cookie Policy.
